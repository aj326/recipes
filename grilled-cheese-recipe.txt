Ingredients:
 - 1/2 T spoon of butter
 - 2 Slices of bread
 - 1 slice of your fav. kind of cheese


Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter.
  2. place both slices on pan next to each other, place slice of cheese on one of them.
  3. when cheese melts, flip the slice with no cheese on top of the one with cheese.
